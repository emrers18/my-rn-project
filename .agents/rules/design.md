# Design System Narrative: Professional Core

## 1. Visual Identity & Color Philosophy

Our design system is anchored by a deep, trustworthy blue palette that conveys stability and professional expertise. The primary interface is set in a light color mode, utilizing a refined neutral base to ensure maximum readability and a clean aesthetic.

- **Primary Brand Color**: #0052CC, a vibrant corporate blue used for primary actions and key brand moments.
- **Secondary Tones**: #002D72 provides depth for deep interaction states or secondary UI elements.
- **Accent/Tertiary**: #00B4FF introduces a bright, digital-first blue for highlights and informative callouts.
- **Neutral Surface**: #F4F7FA is a soft, cool-tinted neutral used for backgrounds to reduce eye strain while maintaining a crisp look.

## 2. Typography

The type system balances modern geometric clarity with high functional legibility.

- **Headlines**: Plus Jakarta Sans is used for all headings, offering a contemporary, high-energy feel that remains professional.
- **Body & Labels**: Manrope is the workhorse font for body text and labels, chosen for its excellent readability and geometric precision across various screen sizes.

## 3. Shape & Spatial Language

The UI is defined by a balanced approach to density and form, avoiding extremes to ensure broad accessibility.

- **Roundedness**: A moderate setting (Level 2) is applied to all components. This results in soft, professional corners that feel modern but structured, moving away from sharp industrial edges without becoming overly playful.
- **Spacing**: We utilize a standard spacing scale (Level 2). This provides a comfortable amount of whitespace that feels organized and rhythmic, preventing the interface from feeling either cramped or excessively sparse.
